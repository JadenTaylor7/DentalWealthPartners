Declining Profits in the Dental Industry

Dentists are increasingly struggling to maintain the profit margins needed to repay student loans, purchase their own practices, and achieve the lifestyle commonly associated with the profession.

As a result, many new graduates are entering the workforce as associates, often finding it difficult to transition into ownership. This dynamic has contributed to the rapid growth of Dental Service Organizations (DSOs), which further complicates the path to independence for many young dentists.
